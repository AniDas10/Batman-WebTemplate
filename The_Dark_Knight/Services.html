Joker

The Joker was a psychotic anarchist mastermind who, portraying himself as an agent of chaos, rose to power in the criminal underworld by thrusting Gotham City into turmoil and drawing Batman ever closer to crossing the fine line between heroism and vigilantism.

Bane

Bane is a super-assassin. Bane has abnormal physical strength as a result of having undergone experiments involving a derivative of the drug Venom. He became known as "The Man Who Broke the Bat" when he broke Batman's back, forcing Bruce Wayne to give up the Batman persona.

The Riddler

"Step into the light...so the Riddler can see you", The Riddler was one of the trickiest villains faced by Batman. Depicted as a Gotham City criminal mastermind, the Riddler takes delight in incorporating riddles and puzzles into his plots, and leaves them as clues for Batman to solve.

Poison Ivy

Poison Ivy is depicted as an eco-terrorist who is obsessed with botany. She uses toxins from plants and her own bloodstream for her criminal activities to protect the natural environment. She creates love potions that ensnare Batman, Superman, and other strong-willed individuals.

Two-Face

Harvey Dent was Gotham City's district attorney and one of Batman's strongest allies who was hideously scarred, distorting his face and fracturing his mental state, causing him to become Two-Face, a schizoid criminal mastermind obsessed with duality and the number two.

The Scare-Crow

Scarecrow uses specially-designed masks to induce peaceful hallucinations in Batman and his allies. The ScareCrow fell from grace when he became absorbed by his own experiments that dwelled into fear and the incredible effects that it could have on the brain.
